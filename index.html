THE STORY

Every empire started as a seed. Every mountain was climbed one step at a time. Mustard Seed Co. is for the ones building something from nothing — the dreamers who put in the work, the grinders who stay faithful to the process.

Premium clothing for people who plant with faith, nurture with discipline, and reap what they've earned.

USAGE RULES
Protect the brand

✓ Do

Use on solid backgrounds (olive, charcoal, cream, white)

✕ Don't

Place on busy photos without a solid backing

✓ Do

Maintain clear space around the logo (1x seed width)

✕ Don't

Stretch, rotate, or alter the proportions

✓ Do

Use brand colors only — olive, mustard, orange, charcoal, cream

✕ Don't

Add drop shadows, gradients, or outline effects

MINIMUM SIZES
• Full logo (icon + wordmark): 1.5" wide minimum
• Icon only: 0.5" minimum
• Wordmark only: 1" wide minimum
• Embroidery: Icon only below 2" width
